📍 BOOMER'S GOURMET FRIES
THE PROJECT //
Working in a team of four throughout our 2B semester at the University of Waterloo, my team-members and myself were tasked to redesign the website of local Stratford fast food chain, Boomer’s Gourmet Fries. I had the pleasure of working on this project with Zaid Amer, Julia Nassereddine, Nathan von Wistinghausen.

BUSINESS OVERVIEW //
Located in Downtown Stratford (ON), Boomer’s Gourmet Fries is a fast food joint with just one location. They serve specifically burgers and their specialty made with local fresh ingredients.

DESIGN CHOICES //
The redesigned website is a simple one page, continuous scroll website. We chose to do it in one page as the original Boomer’s site had little content that needed to be moved over, as well as because a one page layout for a restaurant makes it very easy and effective for anyone to quickly scroll through and navigate; in very few other websites designs will you be able to display a menu and an image gallery of the food directly above/below each other, all while keeping it organized by section.

Aside from the outdated design, the content, image-wise, was lacking on Boomer’s original website. My group and I decided to make a trip to the restaurant with the intention of taking some photos that we would better be able to integrate into the design.
