Hello! I’m Greg, an experience designer from Canada, currently living in Ottawa.

 

I believe that good design exists in whatever medium is best suited to prioretize inclusive, meaningful, and impactful solutions. It isn’t inherently digital-first, nor is it solely about asthetics and good font choice – as fun as that all that is. Good design is about addressing inneficiencies and inequalities, about leveraging existing systems or creating new ones to improve quality of life, foster togetherness, and to encourage a sustainable long-term mindset in regards to our world, our systems, and our products/services.

 

I got my start in experience design studying Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo’s Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business. My time at the University of Waterloo offered me the opportunity to study in Hong Kong, at City University’s School of Creative Media, where I took classes in human-computer interaction and art history.
