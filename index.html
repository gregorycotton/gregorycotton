about. 

Hello, my name is Greg.

I am a multidisciplinary visual artist with an interest in photography, graphic design, and UX/UI. Currently a student studying Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo (Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business), I have had the opportunity to work in a variety of positions, on both team and individual projects. I have 5+ years of experience working with Adobe's Creative Cloud, and am also proficient in HTML, CSS, and Java.

During my time at the University of Waterloo I have worked with different teams outside of my program, such as on Waterloop's marketing team or as a photographer for UW FARMSA, to name a few. I excel when pushed out of my comfort zone and am always looking to try new things and meet new people.

Get in touch with me to talk about anything; whether you’d like to discuss a potential project or what to cook for dinner tonight I’d love to chat. Feel free to reach out by email (gregac.photo@gmail.com), see what I’m up to on Instagram (@greg.a.c), or view my professional page on LinkedIn (in/gregorycotton).
