Hello, my name is
Gregory Cotton.

I am a multidisciplinary visual artist and creative director with an interest in photography, graphic design, and UX/UI. Currently a student studying Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo (Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business), I have had the opportunity to work in a variety of positions, on both team and individual projects. I have 5+ years of experience working with Adobe's Creative Cloud, and am also proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Java.
