2016-2017

Assorted works, 2016-2017 — My desire to create photographs in a more surrealist style was realized in 2016 and 2017. Over the course of these two years, while consistent stylistically, the themes and contents presented in my work were very erratic (few pieces, such as "Dulcet" may not even contain specific ideas to be communicated but exist more so because I thought they looked cool).
