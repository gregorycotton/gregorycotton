Hello,
I'm Greg.

I am interested in using visual arts and design to communicate ideas and messages, both for others and myself. Between my personal work, collaborations, commissions, and projects I have undertaken during my time as a student at the University of Waterloo, I have had the opportunity to work with a wide variety of mediums. From drawing to photographing, to graphic, web and UX, designing, I have accrued a diverse set of skills and an understanding for many of the needed steps throughout the design process.
