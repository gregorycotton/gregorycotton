📍 Hey, my name is Greg!
I was born in Ottawa, and am currently studying Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo. I love to use photography and design to communicate opinions and ideas, among other things. Throughout my projects I make a concerted effort to create visuals using only my original photographs and drawings (with the exception of photos I have been licensed/requested to use for commercial work). Not only do I have 5+ years of experience working with Adobe's Creative Cloud, I am also profficient in HTML, CSS, and Java (I'm ok-ish at JavaScript too). I speak French, have two cats, and I spend a majority of my free time outside of art and school listening to music and drinking coffee. I excel when pushed out of my comfort zone and am always looking to try new things and meet new people.
