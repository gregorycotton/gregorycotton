To Stay At Home

Creative Direction  —   2020–2021

I've worked as a creative director and graphic designer for a number releases from indie band To Stay At Home. This has involved album artwork, promotional posters and content for upcoming releases and performances, as well as motiongraphics for Spotify canvases.

 

 

 

 

 

Listen To Me (2021).

Album artwork, lyric sheet, promotional artwork, and a Spotify canvas for To Stay At Home's 2021 single 'Listen to Me'. The canvas itself involved doing a bit of learning on how to proccess MRI images (not sure if that's the right terminology) to create the spinning brain graphic. Fun, but confirmation that I do not have a long career in medical imagining ahead of me.

 

 

Stream 'Listen to Me' on Spotify or Apple Music.

 

 

Streetlights (2021).

Album artwork, lyric sheet, promotional artwork, and a Spotify canvas for To Stay At Home's 2021 single 'Streetlights'.

 

 

Stream 'Streetlights' on Spotify or Apple Music.
