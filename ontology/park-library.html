'Park Library' is a digital library I built to house the art and writing of friends and others who make cool stuff. Books are set to audio recordings I take at parks to try and best emulate a 'reading at the park' vibe online.

Complete with generative library card generator (no two cards the same!) and library bulletin board for community members to share messages.

Browse the library at parklibrary.org.
