Misc. Etc. Hong Kong

Visual Art  —   2019

I have always been fascinated by large, high-density cities. One of the most staggering elements of Hong Kong to me is the scale of everything; from the height of the buildings, to the number of people. Misc. Etc. is an abstract documentation of Hong Kong's density, created with hundreds of individual images and designed to look as busy as Hong Kong felt.

 

 

 

 

 

Each image in this set is composed of 50 images I took (200 total). I edited the photos in Lightroom, and then brought them into Photoshop where I manually composited them. This gallery was created during autumn of 2019 in Hong Kong and was displayed at the City University of Hong Kong's School of Creative Media.
