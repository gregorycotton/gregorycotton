Misc Etc.

I have always been fascinated by large, high-density cities. One of the most staggering elements of Hong Kong to me is the scale of everything; from the height of the buildings, to the number of people.

Each image in this set is composed of 50 images I took (200 total). I edited the photos in Lightroom, and then brought them into Photoshop where I manually composited them. This gallery was created during autumn of 2019 in Hong Kong and was displayed at the City University of Hong Kong's School of Creative Media.
