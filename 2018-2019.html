2018-2019

Assorted works, 2018-2019 — 2018 and 2019 saw me move slightly away from the traditional surrealist style I had adapted and more towards mixed media pieces that made use of collage art, computer science, and illustration. My individual continued to address issues that I began to talk about in 2017 such as environmentalism, however they continue to work better viewed as seperate pieces with their own themes and ideas, not a collective gallery (as I did spend time creating more cohesive galleries towards the back and of 2019).
