The Open House
2020
Event, Exhibit

–

The 2020 Open House was an event hosted by Hershel in collaboration with Greg, Daniel, Sam, and others, that married the exhibition of visual art, live music and DJ sets, and the energy of a party. The goal was to section off the house to create distinct vibes depending on what room a visitor found themselves in – there were numerous rooms for the display of art, a room for live acoustic performances, basement space for a more 'club' like setting with a live DJ, and areas for people to socialize in.
