The Open House
2020
Event, Experience


The 2020 Open House was an event hosted by Hershel with support from Daniel, Greg and Sam, amongst others, that combined the exhibition of visual art, live music and DJ sets with the energy of a house party.

My series of four abstract documentary pieces reflecting on urban density, Misc. Etc., were on display.
