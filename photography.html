    Weightless

    2019

     

    ‘Weightless’ is a photo I created during the summer of 2019. After having photographed the base image of the man in the fog, I spent approximately a month sorting through daily newspapers and picking out titles and headlines that caught my eye as ambiguously sad, or disheartening (with or without the appropriate context). I created a collage with these newspaper clippings and a black board before staging, lighting, and photographing it so it the images could properly be composed digitally.

    Amylase

    2018

     

    Despite never taking to maths, I can't help but be in awe of the number of appearances mathematics make in nature. The idea that the nature we know is really just a neatly sorted set of equations is fascinating to me. The above image, titled Amylase was created of photos I took during my travels through Scotland, and screenshots I took of a program that visualized the Mandelbrot set that I was written in Java/Proccessing. My intention was to try and blend "data" with a landscape.
